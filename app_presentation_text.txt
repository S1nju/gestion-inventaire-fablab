App Overview
Your app is a full Inventory Management System for a university/faculty environment.
It manages:
- Inventory items (articles/equipment)
- Organizational structure (Facultes, Services, Bureaux)
- Responsables (people in charge)
- Item movements (assignments, transfers, returns, bureau transfers)
- Printable administrative documents (inventory sheet + transfer receipt)

It supports role-based usage:
- Editor: can create/edit/delete and perform movements
- Viewer: read-only visibility

Printable Outputs (Documents)
Your app produces formal print outputs for administration:

1. Fiche d'inventaire (Inventory Sheet)
- Printable inventory listing
- Supports filtering context (bureau/service/faculty)
- Multi-page print behavior for large tables

2. Bon/Attestation de Transfert (Arabic Transfer Receipt)
- Formal printable transfer document
- Institutional bilingual header
- University logo integrated
- Arabic layout (تصريح باستلام)
- Includes source/destination services, transferred items, inventory numbers, notes
- Signature zones (recipient, sender, secretary)
- Optimized for browser print
